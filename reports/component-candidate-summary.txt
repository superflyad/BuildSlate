BuildSlate Component Candidate Summary
======================================

Purpose
-------
This report summarizes the candidate component research registry in
research/components/component_candidates.yaml and the category status tracker in
research/components/component_selection_status.yaml.

Important boundary
------------------
This is a research-tracking system only. It does not claim or imply final
component selection, procurement approval, vendor qualification, certification,
or production readiness. No category currently has a selected component.

Tracked categories
------------------
- soc: under_review; candidate families include Qualcomm Snapdragon and MediaTek Dimensity.
- memory: under_review; candidate families include SK hynix LPDDR5T and Samsung LPDDR5X.
- storage: under_review; candidate families include KIOXIA UFS and Samsung UFS.
- battery: speculative; candidate suppliers/chemistries require custom-cell validation.
- display: under_review; candidate OLED module families require exact panel research.
- camera: under_review; candidate mobile image-sensor families require module definition.
- pmic: speculative; PMIC research depends on final rail map and SoC architecture.
- wireless_charging: under_review; wireless-power architecture and certification path are open.
- thermal_materials: under_review; graphite heat-spreader materials need stackup validation.

Status vocabulary
-----------------
- speculative: Early lead; insufficient evidence for integration planning.
- under_review: Actively being compared against feasibility requirements.
- shortlisted: Promising candidate pending validation and stakeholder review.
- selected: Final selection approved after validation and sourcing review.
- rejected: Removed from consideration with documented reason.

Current selection state
-----------------------
No candidate is marked selected. The registry intentionally keeps all entries in
speculative or under_review states until vendor documentation, electrical
integration, thermal modeling, mechanical fit, sourcing, and risk review are
complete.

Recommended next steps
----------------------
1. Add evidence links for datasheets, product briefs, developer kits, and vendor
   contacts as they become available.
2. Convert candidate notes into comparable requirements: package size, power,
   thermal limits, interface compatibility, cost, lead time, and sourcing risk.
3. Only promote candidates to shortlisted after measurable fit against BuildSlate
   constraints is documented.
4. Do not mark any component selected until cross-disciplinary review is complete.
