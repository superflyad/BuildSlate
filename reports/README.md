# Generated Engineering Reports

Generated reports in this directory are point-in-time snapshots of BuildSlate's first-pass engineering checks. They are intended to make current assumptions, model outputs, validation results, warnings, and unresolved risks easier to review together.

These reports are **not** production certification, compliance evidence, vendor qualification, or a substitute for measured prototype validation. They summarize early feasibility screening only.

Reports may be regenerated as constants, assumptions, component models, validation scripts, and engineering methods improve. Treat report contents as traceable review artifacts rather than permanent source-of-truth specifications.
