Mitigation summary:
  - memory_packaging_512gb:
    - Replace capacity target with supplier-backed package count, height, bandwidth, and power options.
    - Evaluate smaller memory configurations before coupling feasibility to 512 GB.
  - npu_100_tops_sustained:
    - Separate peak marketing TOPS from sustained local workload performance in reports.
    - Require supplier disclosure and measured workloads before using the target as engineering evidence.
  - sustained_compute_power:
    - Define sustained workload classes and throttle policy before preserving headline compute targets.
    - Re-score runtime and thermal reports after supplier-backed SoC power data is available.
  - battery_volume_capacity_tradeoff:
    - Re-baseline battery capacity against actual pack CAD, swelling allowance, and runtime targets.
    - Evaluate lower-capacity and higher-thickness recovery variants before treating v1R as stable.
  - manufacturability_yield_risk:
    - Keep manufacturability status blocked until tolerance, assembly, serviceability, yield, and supplier package evidence exist.
    - Convert high-pressure package assumptions into prototype build risks before committing to industrial design.
  - thickness_stackup_margin:
    - Replace placeholder z-height allocations with CAD stackup and supplier package drawings.
    - Hold v1R thickness as provisional until tolerance stack and local zone conflicts are resolved.
  - storage_packaging_4tb:
    - Check package height, sustained write heat, availability, and cost against the pocket board area.
    - Keep lower-capacity storage variants available during recovery screening.
  - skin_temperature_target_43c:
    - Treat 43 C as a throttle-policy boundary until prototype thermal maps exist.
    - Screen hot ambient, hand contact, charging overlap, and pocket/bag operating states as separate limits.
  - thermal_module_effectiveness:
    - Screen vapor chamber, graphite, frame-coupled spreader, and TIM options against available z-height.
    - Define a thermal test plan before claiming module adequacy.
  - pcb_routing_density:
    - Perform a placement and escape-route study for SoC, memory, storage, RF, PMIC, and flex interconnects.
    - Reassess memory/storage targets if layer count or keepout pressure becomes excessive.
  - power_delivery_transients:
    - Size regulator, battery, and decoupling assumptions from peak transient workloads, not only average compute power.
    - Require PI simulation or bench transients before accepting the compute envelope.
  - rf_antenna_window_constraints:
    - Reserve antenna keepouts and RF-transparent windows before finalizing chassis material or charging layout.
    - Validate coexistence between rear material, wireless charging, frame conduction, and antenna zones.
  - wireless_charging_packaging:
    - Validate coil, ferrite, rear material, alignment, and charging heat as a coupled package.
    - Consider making wireless charging optional if thickness or thermal margin remains blocked.
